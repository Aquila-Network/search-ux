Step 1: request secret key

Request your secret key from https://aquila.network/index.html#form9-3  

You will receive an email with a secret key.

Step 2: install

Install AquilaX Chrome Extension (works in Chrome, Chromium, Brave, Edge etc.) and pin it: https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/aquila-x/albdahjdcmldbcpjmbnbcbckgndaibnk

Step 3: initial setup

Click on the AquilaX extension. When a pop-up appears, press " Edit Settings ", then “ paste your API key ” and press " Update ".

Step 4: bookmark

Visit your favorite website (to be bookmarked), and press the “ Bookmark ” button.

Bonus step: omnibox search

Type “ !! ” in your address bar and hit “ space ”.

When the AquilaX omnibox appears, type your search query.

That’s all, you are ready to go.
